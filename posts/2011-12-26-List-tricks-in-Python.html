List tricks in Python

written on December 26, 2011

Flattening a list of lists

>>> sum([[1],[2],[3],[4]], [])
[1,2,3,4]

Transposing a list of lists

>>> zip(*[[1,2],[3,4]])
[(1,3),(2,4)]
